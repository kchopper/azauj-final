  • About Us

  • What is Azauj?

    Azauj is a matrimonial matchmaking service available both through its website, Facebook Group and offices where our matchmaking consultants meet you. Keeping in mind that many parents/families prefer traditional modes of interaction, we also maintain offices in Lahore andLondon, and also have representatives in Karachi, Gujranwala and New York.

  • How Long Have You Been In Buisness

    We officially started our service in November, 2015.

    Our team consists of CEO Ms. Sadia Saleem Cheema (MSc. LUMS, Ph.D from the University of Manchester, UK) and CTO Mr. Muhammad Hassan (MSc. LUMS + University of York, UK). For further details, please visit the following link.

  • How are you different from other matrimonial websites, marriage bureaus and matchmakers?
    • Privacy - We mantain your anonimity by keeping your picture and contact details private.

    • Better Matches - We recommend you compatible matches based on your requirements through our computer assisted algorithmic evaluation of compatibility which is followed by an overview by experts to find the nearest *best* possible matches.

    • Available and Responsive - Azauj representatives promptly respond to your queries/requests both online and on call.

    • Global reach - We have profiles from all over the world.

    • Multi-channel - You can reach us via Facebook group, website and private consultancy available at our offices.

    • Azauj Special - We offer a special matrimonial matchmaking service for people with disablilities.

  • Why Azauj

  • To which level you ensure privacy of the candidates’ information?

    We do not give profiles’ listings on our website – which is a common practice amongst traditional matchmakers. At our offices too, your record of pictures and contact detail is not shared with anyone until you give us your permission to share it with a candidate/family of your choice. Our system is a two-phase process. In first phase, we only share profile details without disclosing your picture, address and other identity information.We maintain your anonymity. In the second phase, we exchange picture, address and contact details only after getting permission from both families, which agree to take matter ahead.

  • Why should we trust you?

    Because we respect your requirements and understand that your time and resources are precious. We stay in touch with you and refer only those matches which meet or are closest to your requirements.

  • Are you qualified enough as the role of a matchmaker entails responsibility?

    Our team comprises well educated, progressive and proactive professionals. Technical Experience in website, mobile app system? Experience Business wise? Experience in matchmaking field? We’ve gone through harships personally due to bad service available in Pakistan. We are liberating the end-user who doesn’t need to go to other matchmakers now. Our online system recommends matches.

  • How Do We Work?

  • Do you meet the families of candidates before referring them?

    No. But we are available at our offices in Lahore and UK and encourage the candidates and their families to visit us if they want to.

  • Will you be involved in every step during the procedure?

    Yes. We remain involved to the extent both parties want us to be with them until the marriage ceremony takes place.

  • Will you accompany us on a matchmaking visit/meeting?

    YNo. This is beyond our mandate. However, we arrange your meetings with suitable matches at his/her or your home.

  • To what extent you stay in touch once a match is made?

    We give the couple our blessings and sometimes on invitation, attend the wedding ceremony too.

  • How do you know that the candidates have not provided you wrong/fake information

    We have put in place a basic verification system under which every candidate’s residence, education and job/business information is recorded and verified to some extent. However, it is solely your responsibilty to check the background and other information given by the candidate of your choice.

  • Pricing

  • What are your service charges?

    Sign up at our website is free and with that you will recieve some points for free to contact one or two suitable profiles. However, to unlock more suitable profiles recommended to you by our system, you will have to pay $5/PKR Rs. 500 per profile. Azauj Group on Facebook is also free. However, it is a Ladies Only Group. Men can register their profiles there only through a female member of their family. This service does not involve our consultancy. We only get involved when a problem occurs. The members of this group usually have to find the suitable match themselves from the available pool of profiles for free. However, paid profiles posted by admins consume our consultants’ time. hence, we allow maximum two enquiries for paid profiles free of cost. If you want to send more enquiries through our consultants, register with us through our offices or representatives. For our premium service which involves our matchmaking consultants, we charge a registration fee of Rs. 10,000 per person and Rs. 20,000 each after a match is successfully made and accepted by both families.

  • What are your final charges after a match is made?

    Our Premium Service charges are:
    Rs. 10,000 for registration per profile and
    Rs. 20,000 each after a match is made and accepted by both families.

  • Do you charge extra for/from oversees matches?

    No. Our fee is the same for every client.
